CANTOR NATASHA J. HIRSCHHORN

Cantor Natasha J. Hirschhorn is an accomplished singer, pianist and composer, whose music has been featured at Lincoln Center in New York, at Jewish music festivals, and in congregations across North America. She is Music Director of Congregation Ansche Chesed in New York City, a vice president of Shalshelet (shalshelet.org), and is on the faculties of the Jewish Theological Seminary and of Hebrew Union College. A native of Ukraine, Natasha received her training at Moscow Gnesins' Musical College and the Kiev State Conservatory.
